Finnieston Avenue

Finnieston Avenue is a shared accommodation managed by the University of Glasgow and is located in the West End of Glasgow.

It provides 300 shared and en-suite rooms for both Undergraduate and Post-graduate students pursing degrees at the University of Glasgow.
